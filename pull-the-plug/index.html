Pull the Plug

A one-button climb. The narrator is the AI you're trying to shut down.

What it is

You climb. Six missions, one button. A calm therapeutic AI narrates your progress and tells you the off-switch is just a little further up. By the fourth mission it stops sounding so calm. By the sixth it's lashing out. The last word it says is forty-two.

2D vector. Godot 4. Built solo by a non-coder with Claude doing the typing. Coming to Steam, iOS, and Android.
